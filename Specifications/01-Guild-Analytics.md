# Guild Analytics Dashboard

## Player Activity Overview

Track GM levels, guild tenure, daily raid coupons, and last activity timestamp.
Filter out inactive members for focused analysis.
Support custom date ranges and activity thresholds.

## Guild Performance Metrics

Review results in raids, Territory Battles, and Guild Wars.
Compare performance across events and time periods.
Highlight top-performing squads and key contributors.

## Unit Inventory Report
Count specific units across the guild roster.
Group by rarity, gear level, or role (e.g., tank, healer, DPS).
Identify gaps in unit availability for upcoming events.

## Traveler’s Guide Readiness

Assess players’ progress toward completing the Traveler’s Guide.
Flag members who are close to milestones or falling behind.
Provide summary stats for officers to coordinate support.
